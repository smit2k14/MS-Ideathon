About Deoxys.AI

Deoxys.AI is a platform to efficiently compress videos and bring them to a size such that people in rural certain areas that dont have the best of the best internet connection can take advantage of the amazing resources that the internet makes available at their fingerprints. One way an individual can take ownership of their own learning journey is by employing the abundant resources available online. One such resource is video lectures online. For people with limited access to internet, it is essential to download more information using less internet data. We attempt to make this possible by making a tool for the compression and summarization of videos.
